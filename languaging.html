There are posters for study project ‘Languaging’. The limitations of the project are two colours to use and one font. I tried to create a consistent but flexible visual poster system. To do this, I used a grid and the same image size. The composition of the rulers collapses, which creates a contrast in interaction with the typography. The images used were processed screenshots of video footage of police raids, marginal binges and showdowns mainly from Russia. All posters dedicated to the no longer existed Q Theatre. The visual material has taken from YouTube video screenshots of the accidents, police reports and marginal people parties and bickerings from Russia. Font in use: Neue Haas Text Pro.
